Instructions to import into your project:
Select the desired fbx meshes and go to Asset Menu and click Export Package. Open your project and Import it. Then you’re ready to drag the meshes into your scene or instantiate them.

-All light switches include on/off animation

-Both fahrenheit and celcius styles for all thermostats.  You will have to switch color maps in the materials of these respective objects.

-All items come with specular mapping as the colormap alpha channel.

-All outlet, light switch, and vent materials are fully color changeable from within Unity

-Includes free wall, carpet, and ceiling materials, in the Materials folder.
